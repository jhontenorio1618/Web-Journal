Welcome to BLUE: Understanding Your Emotions

How BLUE Works

  1. Input and Assessment: You can input your daily mood and activities into BLUE, and it will analyze the data to assess your daily patterns.
  2. Wellness Recommendations: Based on the analysis, BLUE provides personalized wellness recommendations to help you improve your mental well-being.
  3. Weekly Mood Tracking: When you log in, BLUE greets you with a personalized message and shows you how you've felt during the week, helping you track your emotions over time.
  4. Emotional Resources: BLUE provides a range of resources for your health and mood. If you're feeling down, you'll find hotlines and helplines for immediate support.
  5. Emojis and Quotes: Express your feelings with emojis, and BLUE will respond with a quote to lift your spirits. For example, if you click on the sad emoji, you'll receive a happy or motivating quote.

Embracing All Emotions

At BLUE, we believe that it's essential to assess all our different emotions rather than push them aside. By understanding and embracing our emotions, we can improve our mental well-being and lead happier, more fulfilling lives.

Join us on this journey of self-discovery and emotional well-being with BLUE!
