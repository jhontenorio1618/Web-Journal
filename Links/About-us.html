The Purpose and Goal of
BLUE

Who We Are

We are a dedicated group of student developers passionately working on BLUE.

Our goal is to create a platform that provides assistance and support to individuals.

Together, we aim to build a community that fosters growth, learning, and empowerment.

“BLUE” is a web application

that allows users to monitor

mental well-being

by taking their input

assessing the daily pattern,

and presenting wellness

recommendations based on results



The web application stands to

challenge the idea that blue

is associated with negative emotions

and encourages embracing and

understanding all emotions

regardless of what they are


The website stands to

reinforce the importance

of assessing ALL

our different emotions

rather than push them to

the back of our minds.
